Description

Singether is a software prototype for a final project of my interface design course, done in collaboration with other 4 SIAT students.

Our objective was to create an interactive online karaoke software for linking and entertaining remote family members and friends.

Process and Outcome

At first, after brainstorming, our team totally came up with 3 software ideas that were valuable to be further developed for final project, and Singether was the one I came up with. We then held a group meeting to analyze each idea's pros and cons. After discussion, we finally decided to take Singether as our final idea and further develop it.

In development stage, my job was to design for the software UI/UX, as well as some of the graphics. While making decisions about the overall software flow, I need to be both logical and user-centered at the same time, in order to balance between a functional usability and fluent user experience.

After the Showcase Competition, Singether was invited to be showed at many other showcases afterwards. Each time it was complimented by lots of visitors, both on the interface deisgn and on the online karaoke idea, which was very encouraging and meaningful to me.
