Momentus Software is currently seeking a Principal Engineer for its development office in Vancouver, BC. This is a permanent and full-time position to the right candidate.

Founded in October 2012, Momentus Software is a Vancouver-based startup specializing in building next-gen apps for mobile and tablet devices.

Here at Momentus, we work with a hand-picked team of brilliant engineers who share our passion for mobile technology. Together, our young team brings to your table, over 40 years of mobile industry experience at tech giants like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple and Motorola.

We’ve had tremendous success in developing Android, iOS applications for Tier-1 customers and looking for candidates to join our family. Our clients include US Fortune 500 companies and some of the hottest start ups in the Silicon Valley. Come join our team!

Requirements

  • A graduate degree in Computer Science or Engineering field is required
  • 10+ years of relevant Software Development experience, a minimum of which 5 years should involve Mobile and Embedded Platforms
  • Experience in both Google Android and Microsoft Windows Mobile Operating Systems. Should have at least 2 published titles in Google Play Store and/or Windows Apps Store
  • Experience in Android Frameworks and middleware. Shipping a phone/tablet device is a big plus.
  • Experience in Graphics frameworks, OpenGL, User Interfaces and Experience (UI/UX) development a big plus
  • Highly self-motivated individual with the ability to present to customers
  • Strong communication, leadership and organization skills. 2+ years of leading/managing technical projects highly desired

Responsibilities

  • Provide system level architecture for multiple software products, including the necessary interfaces between functional disciplines. Develop software as proof-of-concept
  • Lead all engineering activities of the group, from concepts to requirements, risk analysis, risk assessment and mitigation of product solutions
  • Mentor the engineering group and provide leadership and technical direction for new products.Collaborate, and influence cross-functional solutions among Momentus Engineering teams
  • Help with project-management – planning, issue tracking, bug triaging. Own the technical aspects of deliveries and deployment to end customers
