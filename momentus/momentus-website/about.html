Our Story

Founded in October 2012, Momentus Software is a Vancouver-based agency specializing in building next-gen apps for mobile and tablet devices. We are your one-stop development shop for native mobile application development (iOS, Android, Windows Phone) with in-house expertise in User Interface/Experience, Graphics, OpenGL, Embedded Technology, Mobile and Enterprise Security, Analytics and Advertising Integration.

Here at Momentus, we work with a handpicked team of brilliant engineers who share our passion for mobile technology. Together, our experienced team brings to your table years of mobile industry experience – at tech giants like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple and Motorola.

All development is proudly performed on the beautiful West Coast by our teams in Vancouver and the Bay Area.

Leadership Team

Almaz Nanjappa

Chief Executive

Almaz Nanjappa is a Silicon Valley veteran, who has been working with mobile technology since its inception. Almaz recently led the Electronic-Paper initiatives at NOOK for its ever popular NOOK e-Readers. Prior to that, Almaz led the Mobile Client team at Adobe Systems where he pioneered several initiatives working with mobile giants like Qualcomm, T-Mobile, Nokia and Research In Motion. Almaz, a proud Badger, holds a degree in Computer Science and Math from University of Wisconsin-Madison.

David Bolcsfoldi

Chief Technology Officer

David Bolcsfoldi is a Systems Engineer with over 10 years of experience with focus on embedded operating systems. David has led teams developing software for a range of embedded platforms such as Android, standard Linux, Symbian and various custom RTOSs. With a keen interest in the embedded system software sphere, David has successfully delivered multiple consumer and enterprise embedded solutions, most notably the Barnes & Noble NOOK line of e-Readers and tablets. Although most comfortable at the system levels he has dabbled in applications for most of the major operating systems and has held presentations at numerous conferences, most recently LinuxCon.

Soren Silkenson

Director of Business Development

Soren Silkenson leads all business development and marketing efforts for Momentus. He has years of experience building successful partnerships with enterprise clients, SMBs, and startups. He’s passionate about helping companies solve complex problems through the use of technology. When he’s not building long-term partnerships with clients you can find him designing and developing video games and VR experiences.

Anindya Roy

Chief Operating Officer

Anindya Roy represents our Silicon Valley operations. With 12+ years of experience developing Desktop and Mobile software, consumer and prosumer alike, Anindya has always tried to bridge design and engineering faculties. Prior to Momentus, Anindya wrote type and rendering subsystems on nine generations of the Barnes & Noble ‘nook’ e-readers. He has a keen eye for the present and future of mobile and wearable technology and specializes in defining and architecting beautiful software products with a intense focus on meaningful user-experiences and performant interfaces. Anindya holds a graduate degree in Computer Science from Columbia University, New York.

Nick Rindahl

Director of Customer Development

Nick Rindahl is a Customer Development Specialist whose passion lies in alleviating customer pains through elegant software solutions and products. With years of customer-facing experience, Nick has learned that Customer Development isn’t about asking customers what they want, it’s about seeking to understand what they need, how they work, and where their highest priorities are.

Adam Wilson

Director of Design

Adam Kyle Wilson speaks for design at Momentus. With a background rooted in industrial and product design, Adam has been designing experiences for iOS and Android for close to 5 years. His work has been featured by the likes of Time Magazine (top 50 apps of 2013), Bloomberg Businessweek , Forbes, Fast Company, Tech Crunch, and even showcased on-stage at the 2014 Google I/O. Dan Burgar

Business Development Manager
