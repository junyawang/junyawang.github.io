Momentus Software is currently seeking a Principal Engineer for its development office in Vancouver, BC. This is a permanent and full-time position to the right candidate.

Founded in October 2012, Momentus Software is a Vancouver-based startup specializing in building next-gen apps for mobile and tablet devices.

Here at Momentus, we work with a hand-picked team of brilliant engineers who share our passion for mobile technology. Together, our young team brings to your table, over 40 years of mobile industry experience at tech giants like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple and Motorola.

We’ve had tremendous success in developing Android, iOS applications for Tier-1 customers and looking for candidates to join our family. Our clients include US Fortune 500 companies and some of the hottest start ups in the Silicon Valley. Come join our team!

Requirements

  • 4+ years of professional experience managing technology projects
  • Strong history and track record of completing quality projects on time and on budget
  • Familiarity with Mobile Application Frameworks and/or Systems Architecture
  • Knowledge and practice in software development methodologies such as Agile, Scrum, Lean UX, and/or Waterfall
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate with cross-functional and distributed teams, including designers, developers and QA engineers
  • Excellent written and verbal communication and organizational skills
  • Experience operating in an agency and/or a services firms is a plus

Responsibilities

  • Software Project-management focussed on Mobile/Web application development – planning, issue tracking, bug triaging
  • Manage software product deliveries and deployment to end customers
  • Expertly handle all aspects of JIRA including Sprint planning, issue tracking, integrations with CI systems, user/team management
  • Scheduling, Road-mapping and Time-tracking - all leading to ensure high-quality products within committed timeframes and budgets.
  • Manage Customer interactions, be a responsible front for the company and the project to customers
