Founded in October 2012, Momentus Software is a Vancouver-based startup specializing in building next-gen apps for mobile and tablet devices.

Here at Momentus, we work with a hand-picked team of brilliant engineers who share our passion for mobile technology. Together, our young team brings to your table, over 40 years of mobile industry experience – at tech giants like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple, Motorola and so on.

We’ve had tremendous success in developing Android, iOS applications for Tier-1 customers and looking for candidates to join our family. Our clients include US Fortune 500 companies and some of the hottest start ups in the Silicon Valley. Come join our team!

About the Role

As our intermediate / senior iOS engineer, you make sure our work meets the highest level of quality and not afraid to review and take criticisms. You cringe and speak out when something does not meet your expectation and you make simple solutions to complex problems.

Skills & Experience

  • Passionate in mobile development and related technologies
  • Excellent knowledge of iOS 7, UI Kit and expert in Core Data, Core Graphics
  • Strong understanding of memory management, file I/O, network programming, concurrency and multi-threading in mobile development
  • Have already gone through WWDC iOS 8 videos and has a lot to say about Swift
  • Have memorized human interface guidelines from Apple
  • Have apps in the app store to impress us
  • Independent! We expect you to take ownership of the project

Bonus

  • Previous product development experience
  • Android / Windows Mobile experience
  • BSP, OpenGL experience is a plus
