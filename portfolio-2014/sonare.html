Description

Sonare is an iOS based mobile application that provides a more humanized and efficient way for users to encounter potential employers/employees in public space and exchange recruitment/resume information digitally. It is a concept we develop for the "Creatives of the Year Competition" in SIAT, SFU. This competition was held for celebrating SIAT’s 10th Anniversary in 2013.

Process and Outcome

This competition aims teams to offer teams an opportunity to come up with projects that revolve around the theme of social innovation within 2 weeks. It encourages us to use our knowledge from SIAT to create technologies that will benefit our daily life as well as creating social values and impacts.

Our team spent a whole afternoon in our first meeting and decided our project concept. Facing such a tight deadline, we had a problem about whether to reinvent an existing technology or to create our own design based on the ideas we came up with. Surprisingly, all of us voted for our best idea because we were really passionate about it and agreed it was more valuable to develop. Therefore, we finally decided to develop our own application that will change the way which people used to recruit and job-hunting, as well as exchanging personal information.

As the UI/UX designer in the team, my goal was to create a user-friendly interface and user-centered experience for our target audience. With that in mind, I created the UI for this app as well as some mockups to demonstrate what the application looks like. We also created an interactive prototype as a live demo to show how the application works.

As a result, our project was nominated as one of the Top 5 teams in the competition.
