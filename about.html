about me.

Hi, I'm Junya Wang, a UI/UX + graphic designer and front-end developer in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. I just graduated from School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University in 2014 Summer. SIAT is an interdisciplinary future-focused school for technologists, artists and designers that concentrate on the study of interactive design, incorporating aesthetics with technical skills. Studying in SIAT provides me an opportunity to go through various project work, gain experience mainly in graphic, interface and interaction design.

Being professional on designing, programming and applying design theories simultaneously, I can be a combination of graphic, interface and interaction designer. Being creative, having a strong sense of color and taste on style, I am good at designing and uniting the style of my work. Being patient, responsible and good at precision, even details of the project needed to be done fastidiously. With strong technical, organization skill and comprehension, I am able to function more efficiently as a team member.
